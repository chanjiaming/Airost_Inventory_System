What is Inventory System?

The inventory system at Universiti Teknologi Malaysia's AIROST Team efficiently manages and tracks hardware resources using advanced tools and processes. This system ensures meticulous organization and monitoring of diverse technological assets, optimizing operational efficiency, minimizing resource wastage, and streamlining overall technological infrastructure management. It contributes significantly to the success of the team's cutting-edge research in artificial intelligence and robotics.

Why Inventory system is important?

An inventory system is essential for Universiti Teknologi Malaysia AIROST Team to efficiently manage and track its components, tools, and equipment. It ensures resource allocation, saves time through organization, facilitates activity planning, and promotes accountability among team members.
